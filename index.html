About Me

I'm Wassim Slimane, a Full-Stack Developer driven by a passion for creating robust applications and crafting user-centric digital experiences. My journey in tech has taken me through dynamic roles at companies like RECRUTIMMO, DIGIPLACE, and PREPACADEMY, where I've honed my skills in both front-end and back-end development. Holding a Master’s degree in Web Development from HITEMA and a Bachelor’s in Computer Science from the University of Paris-Saclay, with international experience at the University of Memphis, I bring a well-rounded, global perspective to my work.


Coding is more than just work for me; it's where I find joy in turning creative ideas into functional and beautiful applications. I love tackling complex problems and continuously expanding my skill set to keep up with the latest tech trends. Every day, I’m motivated to build solutions that make a meaningful impact and bring real value to users.
